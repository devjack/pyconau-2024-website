# PyCon AU, Australia's peak Python conference

In 2024, PyCon AU will be held from the **22nd to the 26th of November** at the **Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre (MCEC) in Narrm/Melbourne**.

Keynoting PyCon AU will be **Linda McIver**, presenting [From Hypnotised to Heretic: Immunising Society Against Misinformation](/program/BXZ9ZA), and **Kendra Vant**, presenting [The neglected art of a relevant benchmark and why you need one](/program/WDC77B).
